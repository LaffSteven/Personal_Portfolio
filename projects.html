GA Protfolio Project

My first project here at GA!
I'm building a portfolia site, on which I ca host my future GA Projects, and possibly use moving forward. I'm using a combination of HTML 5, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery to display interactive and scalable content. I hope you like this site, and the fun little interactions you can have with the content.
The site should scale separately for desktop and mobile, although the interactive parts will be a bit different on either platform. Some interactions don't look as good on mobile.
